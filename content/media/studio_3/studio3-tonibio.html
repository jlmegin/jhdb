Ballard, Toni, voice; born. Chicago, Illinois.  Sang chorus in school; learned guitar in college; formed a band with her husband. Hosted WICN Radio show “Tasteful Blend” 1981-1984; Recorded a children’s jazz album 1985; Recorded I’m Your Pal with Larry Coryell 1995; Produced and hosted CableACE nominated Studio 3 Jazz show 1993-2000; publicist for Berklee School of Music 1998-2004.  Is responsible for forming Berklee and BET (Black Entertainment Television) relationship, worked on Jazz Scene and Impressions.  Has performed with The Silver Bullet Orchestra, the Nelson Riddle Orchestra, the Boston Big Band, the White Heat Swing Orchestra, Ryles Jazz Orchestra, Worcester Jazz Orchestra, Benny Goodman Tribute Band  Now is a freelance publicist, and is involved with benefit concerts for Pancreatic Cancer Alliance.
