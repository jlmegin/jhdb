About Studio 3

Studio 3 is a collection of more than 90 TV shows featuring jazz artists in concert and interviews. Shot in the studios of WGMC TV and in such prestigious locations as Mechanics Hall, Jazz at Sunset Festival, Mass Jazz Festival and Jazz Worcester Festival, these videos contain unique footage never seen before. Local and international artists include Larry Coryell, Herb Pomeroy, Dick Johnson, Cecilia Smith, James Williams, Emil Haddad and Karrin Allyson.

Episodes are reproduced here with the express permission of WGMC TV and Toni Ballard, executive producer and host of Studio 3.
